# HashFix

Automatic hashtag formatting for your emails

Status: Active

HashFix is monitoring your outgoing emails and will automatically fix improperly formatted hashtags before sending.

What HashFix Does:

  • Fixes "# tag" → "#tag"
  • Fixes "# tag" → "#tag" (multiple spaces)
  • Works with numbers: "# 123" → "#123"
  • Preserves email formatting
  • Runs automatically when you send emails
